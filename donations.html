Keisha's Krew was created by Keisha Curvey's family and friends to carry on her beautiful legacy as a beacon of hope and goodwill within our communities. Keisha has made a positive impact in our community as a top notch educator which will last forever. As we celebrate her life, we want to be an extension of the kindness and positive impact she made. The money raised will go to the Kidney Foundation and establish the scholarship award for the years to come.Please share this page! We really appreciate your support. Thank you.
